Citizenship

I believe that honesty, compassion, respect, responsibilty,courage and accountable are necessary components to being a good citzenship

*Honesty is demenstrated when somebody tells the complete truth in any situation.

*Compassion is the ability to understand the emotional state of another person or oneself.

*Showing respect to someone means you act in a way that shows you care about their feelings and well-being.

*Being responsible means you do the things you are expected to do and accept the consequences of your actions.

*Courage is the ability to do something that frightens one.

*Being accountable means that you can be trusted to get the job done when asked and that you can be trusted.
